# 混合运动现象汇总

**1. 2个物体 - 简谐运动(弹簧)-碰撞现象-平面运动**

- **description:**
    压缩的弹簧释放物体A，物体A被弹出后在水平面上做匀速直线运动，与静止的物体B发生弹性碰撞，碰撞后物体A静止，物体B在平面上滑动。
    *(Object A is ejected by a compressed spring, moves in uniform straight motion on a horizontal surface, and undergoes an elastic collision with stationary object B, after which object A stops and object B slides on the surface.)*

**2. 2个物体 - 非线性摆运动-碰撞现象-自由落体**

- **description:**
    双摆在摆动过程中（混沌状态），下端的摆球撞击位于高处边缘的物体A，导致物体A失去支撑并由静止开始做自由落体运动。
    *(During the chaotic swing of a double pendulum, the lower bob collides with object A positioned on a high edge, causing object A to lose support and fall freely from rest.)*

**3. 1个物体 - 圆周运动-推拉现象-抛射运动**

- **description:**
    物体在绳子的牵引力作用下在水平桌面上做圆周运动，随后绳子被剪断（外力消失），物体沿切线方向以抛射运动飞出桌面。
    *(An object undergoes circular motion on a horizontal table under the traction of a rope, then the rope is cut, and the object flies off the table in projectile motion along the tangent.)*

**4. 1个物体 - 浮力现象-平面运动-斜面运动**

- **description:**
    物体从水底受浮力作用上浮，冲出水面后在水面上滑行一段距离，随后依靠惯性冲上连接在水边的斜面。
    *(An object rises from underwater due to buoyancy, rushes out of the water surface, slides on the water for a distance, and then rushes up an inclined plane connected to the water's edge due to inertia.)*

**5. 2个物体 - 简谐运动(单摆)-碰撞现象-物体自转-斜面运动**

- **description:**
    单摆摆下时撞击静止的球体A，球体A获得速度并开始旋转，随后带着旋转沿斜面向上滚动。
    *(A pendulum swings down and collides with a stationary sphere A, causing sphere A to gain speed and start spinning, after which it rolls up an inclined plane.)*

**6. 1个物体 - 物体自转-自由落体-碰撞现象-平面运动**

- **description:**
    一个高速旋转的球体从高处做自由落体运动，撞击水平地面后，由于旋转产生的摩擦力，反弹时产生水平方向的速度，并在平面上跳跃前进。
    *(A high-speed spinning sphere falls freely from a height, hits the horizontal ground, and due to the friction generated by rotation, rebounds with a horizontal velocity component and skips forward on the plane.)*

**7. 2个物体 - 平面运动-碰撞现象-简谐运动(弹簧)**

- **description:**
    物体A在光滑平面上滑行，撞击连接着弹簧的挡板（物体B），两者结合在一起压缩弹簧，随后开始做简谐运动。
    *(Object A slides on a smooth surface and collides with a baffle (object B) connected to a spring, sticking together to compress the spring and then beginning simple harmonic motion.)*

**8. 1个物体 - 抛射运动-浮力现象-物体自转**

- **description:**
    旋转的物体被以一定角度抛入水中，入水后下沉减速，随后在浮力作用下边旋转边上浮至水面。
    *(A spinning object is projected into water at an angle, decelerates while sinking, and then floats up to the surface due to buoyancy while continuing to spin.)*

**9. 2个物体 - 斜面运动-碰撞现象-非线性摆运动**

- **description:**
    物体A从斜面上滑下，撞击静止悬挂的双摆的上端摆球，导致双摆系统开始进行混沌摆动。
    *(Object A slides down an inclined plane and collides with the upper bob of a stationary suspended double pendulum, causing the double pendulum system to start chaotic swinging.)*

**10. 2个物体 - 推拉现象-平面运动-碰撞现象-圆周运动**

- **description:**
    物体A受推力在平面上加速，撞击通过绳索拴在柱子上的物体B，物体B获得速度后围绕柱子做圆周运动。
    *(Object A is accelerated by a pushing force on a plane and collides with object B, which is tethered to a pillar by a rope, causing object B to gain speed and perform circular motion around the pillar.)*

**11. 2个物体 - 简谐运动(单摆)-碰撞现象-简谐运动(弹簧)**
